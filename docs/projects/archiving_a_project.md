#Archiving a Project


Follow the steps below to archive a project.

1. From your projects list, click the project you would like to archive. At the top of your project page, click the **edit button** or you can click on the name of the project in the menu bar and select **Edit Project Information**. This will bring you to the *Edit Project* page. <p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/archiving_a_project_1_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 1"> </p>  <p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/archiving_a_project_2_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 1"> </p>

2. Scroll to the bottom of the *Edit Project* page. Click the black **archive button**, it looks like a file box within a circle.  <p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/creating_a_project_3_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 2"> </p>


3. A popup will appear asking you to confirm if you would like to archive the project. Click the **Archive Project** button. This will move your project from your projects list to the archived projects list.  <p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/archiving_a_project_4_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 3"> </p>


##Unarchive a Project

1. From your project list page, click on the **Archived** tab option, located above the *Create New Project* button. <p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/archiving_a_project_5_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 1"> </p>

2. This will bring you to a page listing all of your archived projects. Find the project you would like to *Unarchive* and click the **Unarchive** button located in the bottom right corner of the project box. <p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/archiving_a_project_6_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 2"> </p>

This will automatically remove the project from the Archived list, and move it back to your *Custom* and *Alphabetical* active project lists. You will recieve a pop-up notification in the top-right corner of your webpage informing you that your project was successfully unarchived.
