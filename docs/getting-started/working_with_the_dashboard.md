
# Working with the Dashboard

The dashboard is your user interface that provides at-a-glance views of your projects and blocks. You can use the dashboard to quickly navigate to your most recent work in Kora. 

You can modify your Dashboard, allowing you to custimize your dashboard to what works best for you, or you can hide your dashbaord completely. 

## Enable/Disable Dashboard

1. When you are logged into Kora, you can access your user preferences from anywhere. Click on the person icon in the top menu located in the top right corner of the page. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_1_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 1"> </p>

2. This will open a dropdown menu. Choose the **My Preferences** option. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_2_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 2"> </p>

3. On the **My Preferences** page, you are able to modify your kora preferences. There are four different preferences you can modify. This is where you can chose to enable or disable your dashboard.  

4. The dashboard option allows you to choose whether you would like to use the dashboard or to turn off the dashboard entirely. Click the circle next to the option you would like to select, this will change the color of the circle and text to blue and add a white checkmark within the circle. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_3_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="use dashboard"> </p>

5. When you have finished making changes to your kora user preferences, click the **Update Preferences* blue button at the bottom of the page to save the changes. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_4_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 4"> </p>

## Dashboard Sections

You can organze your dashboard into sections. This can allow you to put blocks (projects, forms, records, quotes, notes, and a Kora Twitter feed) into different areas. 

### Adding Sections to your Dashboard

1. Select edit your dashboard. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_27_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 1"> </p>

2. Scroll to the bottom of your dashboard until you find **Type Here and Hit Enter to Add a New Section**. Type in your section title and then hit enter on your keyboard to add the section to your dashboard. You can add as many sections as you would like during this step. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_35_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 2"> </p>


3. Click **Finish Editing Dashboard** to exit the editing of your dashboard either at the top or bottom of the page. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_33_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 3"> </p><p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_34_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 3"> </p>

### Removing Sections from your Dashboard

1. Select edit your dashboard. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_27_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 1"> </p>

2. Scroll until you find the section you would like to remove and click the **X** on the right side of the screen. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_36_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 2"> </p>


3. Click **Finish Editing Dashboard** to exit the editing of your dashboard either at the top or bottom of the page. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_33_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 3"> </p><p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_34_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 3"> </p>


## Dashboard Blocks 

Blocks are shortcuts you can add to your dashboard sections. These can be shortcuts to projects, forms, records, notes, twitter feed from Kora, and auto-generated quotes.


### Adding Blocks to your Dashboard

1. Navigate to your dashboard and select **Add New Block** <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_5_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 1"> </p>

2. This will open a popup. <p align="center"><img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_6_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 2"> </p>


3. Here you can decide what you would like to add to your block including project, form, record, quote, kora twitter, and a note. See the following sub-sections for detailed instruction for each block type option. <p align="center"><img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_7_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 3"> </p>


4. Following your selection of block type and section, click the **Add New Dashboard Block** button. <p align="center"><img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_8_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 4"> </p>

## Editing Quick Access Actions

The quick actions in Kora are the six icons on the bottom of each block in your dashboard. 

If you want to rearrange the order of **Quick Action** items, then follow these steps:

1. Select edit your dashboard. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_27_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 1"> </p>


2. Hover your mouse over the block whose **Quick Actions** you wish to edit. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_28_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 2"> </p>

3. Select **Edit Quick Actions**. This will prompt the *Edit Quick Actions* pop-up to appear. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_29_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 3"> </p>

4. You may edit the order of quick actions by either pressing the **up arrow** or **down arrow**. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_30_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 4"> </p> Alternatively, you may also click on the **Quick Action** you want to reorder, and drag it to its desired location. 

5. Note that only the top 6 **Quick Actions** will be displayed in the block that you are editing. The followign three will appear when you hover over the far right <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_31_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 5"> </p>

6. Click **Update Quick actions** to save your changes. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_32_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 6"> </p>

7. Click **Finish Editing Dashboard** to exit the editing of your dashboard either at the top or bottom of the page. <p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_33_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 7"> </p><p align="center"> <img src="../getting-started-img/working_with_the_dashboard_34_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 7"> </p>
