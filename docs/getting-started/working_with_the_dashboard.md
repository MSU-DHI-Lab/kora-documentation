## Editing the Order of Quick Access Actions
If you want to rearrange the order of **Quick Action** items, then follow these steps:

1. Select edit your dashboard

2. Hover your mouse over the block whose **Quick Actions** you wish to edit.

3. Select **Edit Quick Actions**. This will prompt the *Edit Quick Actions* pop-up to appear.

4. You may edit the order of quick actions by either pressing the **up arrow** or **down arrow**. Alternatively, you may also click on the **Quick Action** you want to reorder, and drag it to its desired location.

5. Note that only the top 6 **Quick Actions** will be displayed in the block that you are editing.

6. Click **Update Quick actions** to save your changes

7. Click **Finish Editing Dashboard** to exit the editing of your dashboard.

## Adding Projects to your Dashboard

Adding a project to your dashboard is the [same process as adding any block to your dashboard](../getting-started/working_with_the_dashboard.md):

1. Navigate to your dashboard and select **Add New Block** <p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/managing_projects_from_the_dashboard_1_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 1"> </p>

2. Select "project" from the **Dashboard Block type** drop-down menu.<p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/managing_projects_from_the_dashboard_2_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 2"> </p>

3. Select the project that you want to appear on your dashboard from the dashboard menu <p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/managing_projects_from_the_dashboard_3_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 3"> </p>

4. Once you've selected the project for your block, you have the option to choose what section of your dashboard that you want the block to appear in. <p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/managing_projects_from_the_dashboard_4_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 4"> </p>

5. Click **Add New Dashboard Block** <p align="center"> <img src="../projects-img/managing_projects_from_the_dashboard_5_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 5"> </p>
