#Edit User Account

You can edit your kora user account profile. 

Follow the steps below to edit your user preferences.

1. When you are logged into Kora, you can access your user preferences from anywhere. Click on the person icon in the top menu located in the top right corner of the page. <p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_1_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 1"> </p>

2. This will open a dropdown menu. Choose the **Edit My Profile** option. <p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_2_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 2"> </p>

3. On your **Profile** page, you are able to modify your kora user account profile information. There are four different preferences you can modify. 
	* Edit your User Name
	* Change your email address
	* Edit your first and last name
	* Upload a profile image
	* Change your Organization
	* Select the language used
	* Update Your Password
 
	You can view more information about each of these options below. <p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_3_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 3"> </p>

4. When you have finished making changes to your kora user account profile, click the **Update Profile* blue button at the bottom of the page to save the changes. <p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_5_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step 4"> </p>

##Edit Your User Name
You can edit your username by typing in the white box below the **User Name** sub-heading. <p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_5_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Step Edit Your User Name"> </p>


##Change Email Address
You can edit your email address by typing in the white box below the **Email Address** sub-heading.<p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_6_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Change Email Address"> </p>


##Edit First and Last Name
You can edit your first and last name by typing in the white box below the **First Name** & **Last Name** sub-heading.<p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_7_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Edit First and Last Name"> </p>


##Upload a Profile Image
You can upload or change your profile photo under the **Profile Image** sub-heading. To do this, you can either drag and drop a photo from your computer or click the **Drag and Drop or Select a Photo here** option. After you select and image or drag & drop a photo from your computer, it will upload and show the preview of the image within the circle. You have the option to delete this photo by clicking on the **'X'** next to the image file name. You can also select a different photo by clicking the **Select a Different Photo?** option<p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_8_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Upload Profile Image"> </p>


##Edit Your Organization
You can edit your organization by typing in the white box below the **Organization** sub-heading. <p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_9_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Edit Your Organization"> </p>


##Select Language Used
Currently, English is the only supported language. Future versions of Kora will support other languages. 

To change the language used on Kora, click the white bar below the **Language** sub-heading. <p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_10_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Select Language Used"> </p>


##Update Password
You can update your kora user password under the **Update Password** sub-heading. You must first enter your new password in the white box under **Enter New Password** section. Then confirm your new password by typing the new password again in the white box under **Confirm New Password**.<p align="center"> <img src="../user-accounts-img/edit_user_account_11_annotated.png" width="100%" style="align:center" title="Update Password"> </p>
